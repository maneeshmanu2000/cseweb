About Us

The International Conference on Energy and Environmental Engineering is a multi-disciplinary, peer-reviewed international conference on sustainable energy and environment. The objective of this conference is to bring together researchers from academia, government agencies, research laboratories and the corporate sector to present their research works carried out in energy production and management, green energy, environmental engineering, environmental management, climate change and sustainable development.
